<h4>Transport</h4>
<p>Due to closure of certain roads and lanes around Marina Bay on New Year’s
Eve, the public is encouraged to make&nbsp;their way to MBSC 2025 via the
island-wide Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) system. Train services will be extended
on 31 December 2024. You may check the&nbsp;<a href="http://www.smrt.com.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SMRT</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a href="https://www.sbstransit.com.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SBS Transit</a>&nbsp;websites
for the latest changes in operating hours.</p>
<p>All MRT lines (East-West Line, North-South Line, Downtown Line, Circle
Line and Thomson-East Coast Line) have stops in the Marina Bay area. The
nearest stations are:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Marina Bay (NS27/TE20/CE2)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Raffles Place (NS26/EW14)</p>
</li>
<li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
