<h4>Transport</h4>
<p>Due to closure of certain roads and lanes around Marina Bay on New Year’s
Eve, the public is encouraged to make&nbsp;their way to MBSC 2025 via the
island-wide Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) system. Train services will be extended
on 31 December 2024. You may check the&nbsp;<a href="http://www.smrt.com.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SMRT</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a href="https://www.sbstransit.com.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SBS Transit</a>&nbsp;websites
for the latest changes in operating hours.</p>
<p>All MRT lines (East-West Line, North-South Line, Downtown Line, Circle
Line and Thomson-East Coast Line) have stops in the Marina Bay area. The
nearest stations are:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Marina Bay (NS27/TE20/CE2)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Raffles Place (NS26/EW14)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>City Hall (NS25/EW13)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shenton Way (TE19)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Downtown (DT17)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bayfront (DT16/CE1)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promenade (DT15/CC4)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Esplanade (CC3)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Gardens by The Bay (TE22)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>For public safety and to facilitate crowd management, the number of people
entering and leaving key areas around the Bay and its surrounding districts
will be regulated on New Year’s Eve. These areas will be closed when their
capacity limits are reached. The public can visit the&nbsp;<strong><a href="https://experience.arcgis.com/experience/afc51853c10143a4a7adc5a0b09ad4b2/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Crowd@MarinaBay map</u></a></strong>&nbsp;for
timely updates on the crowd situation on New Year’s Eve and plan your routes
and activities accordingly.
